### Troubleshooting

---

**Q: The product is not foaming. What should I do?**

A: Flush or clear excess standing water before use — too much water dilutes the formula. Make sure you are using the full packet per application. Let it sit undisturbed for 5–10 minutes to allow full activation. Optionally, follow up with a flush of warm (not boiling) water to boost the reaction.

---

**Q: There is residue left after use.**

A: Do not use more than one packet at a time — excess powder may not fully dissolve. Flush twice or rinse with warm (not hot) water after sit time. If residue remains, gently scrub with a toilet brush before final rinse.

---

**Q: The stains are still there after one use.**

A: For stubborn or old stains, you may need to repeat the application 1–2 more times over a few days. After each application, gently agitate with a soft-bristled toilet brush to lift loosened residue.

---

**Q: The powder is clumping in the packet.**

A: Gently shake or tap the packet to loosen clumped powder — this does not affect cleaning power. Store packets in a cool, dry place. Humidity causes clumping. Keep packets sealed tightly between uses.

---

**Q: The smell is overpowering.**

A: The scent is Ocean Fresh fragrance — the product is bleach-free and ammonia-free. Ventilate the bathroom by turning on the exhaust fan, cracking a window, or leaving the door open during and after use.

---

**Q: The product irritated my skin.**

A: The product is bleach-free, but gloves are recommended for sensitive skin. Use a scoop or spoon to avoid touching the powder directly. If skin contact occurs, rinse immediately with cool water and mild soap. Use in a well-ventilated space to reduce airborne powder exposure.

---

**Q: My toilet clogged after using the product.**

A: The product dissolves safely in water and should not cause clogs when used correctly. Use only 1 full packet per use. After the sit time, flush with warm (not boiling) water. Do not mix with bleach or other cleaners, as this may cause clumping or chemical reactions.
